Sometimes, combobox's element can not be selected, you can use Actions to do it instead

WebElement webElement = driver.findElement(By.xpath(prop.getProperty("cowboyhat_size_select")));
	  
	  Actions actions = new Actions(driver);
	  actions.moveToElement(webElement);
	  actions.click();
	  actions.sendKeys(Keys.ARROW_DOWN);
	  actions.build().perform();
